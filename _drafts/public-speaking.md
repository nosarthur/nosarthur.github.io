The art of public speaking


The efficiency of a book is like that of a man, in one important respect: its attitude toward its subject is the first source of its power.

key ideas

* The man should be and think and feel things that are worthy of being given forth
* The man must enthrone his will to rule over his thought, his feelings,
  and all hid physical powers, so that the outer self may give perfect, unhampered
  expression to the inner.
* No one can learn how to speak who does not first speak as best he can.
